## ACT STRUCTURE

**Act I (Ch 1-6, 0-23%):** Ninsar's world of loss and memory-work. We see her hollowing herself to preserve her daughter's face. The Catalyst: a summons to map the final coast. The Debate: should she take the commission? Break Into Two: she accepts, leaves the salt-flat city.

**Act II Part 1 (Ch 7-12, 23-50%):** Journey to the shrinking sea. Ninsar meets her crew, encounters the ocean's bioluminescent language, begins to suspect the sea is sentient and fleeing. Midpoint: she witnesses the ocean speak a word directly to herˇŞand understands it.

**Act II Part 2 (Ch 13-18, 50-77%):** The ocean's pattern becomes readable. Ninsar learns the old gods beneath the sea-floor are dreaming, and the ocean's retreat is a protective contraction. Her daughter's memory is the price of understanding. All Is Lost: she must choose between preserving that memory and learning the full truth.

**Act III (Ch 19-24, 77-100%):** Ninsar makes her choice. The climax involves a final memory-transfer that costs her everything she has left. Resolution shows her hollow but freeˇŞor full but trappedˇŞdepending on the choice.
